ABOUT

Founded by three childhood friends, SaunaGuus was born from a passion for transforming Copenhagen’s harsh, dark winters into moments of warmth, connection, and renewal.

In Denmark, we’ve always had the sauna — a place to warm the body and clear the mind. But it wasn’t until we discovered the power of sharing that experience in community that everything changed. Cold days no longer kept us apart; they drew us together. As the days grew colder and shorter, winter transformed from isolation into celebration.

Through SaunaGuus, we now look forward to the cold — even in the darkest months.

OUR SPARK

SaunaGuus was born from a simple belief: that the ritual of saunagus shouldn’t be reserved for the few, but shared with everyone.

In the Scandinavian tradition, saunagus has always been about more than heat. It is rooted in centuries of culture and community — not the trappings of white robes, exclusive memberships, or unaffordable day passes. Here, the practice is shaped by authenticity, not by borrowed notions of luxury.

Our mission is to share this tradition openly and honestly — whether you are an athlete, a student, or simply someone in need of a reset. Everyone can enter, everyone can belong. SaunaGuus is not a club — it’s a sanctuary. A place where heat, cold, and scent meet presence and attention. A place where you don’t need to prove anything, perform, or follow a trend. Whether it’s your first session or your hundredth, you’ll find space to slow down and reconnect — stripped of noise, stripped of demand.

ETHOS

In a world that demands constant motion, SaunaGuus carves out a space for complete
composure — a space to disconnect from demands and reconnect with yourself.

At SaunaGuus, we offer something rooted in centuries of Scandinavian tradition: the ritual of saunagus. Through heat, essential oils, breathwork, and cold exposure, we guide you into release — physically, mentally, and emotionally.

This is not about performance.
It’s about presence.

Each session is an invitation to stop pushing, to soften, and to feel. Not a test of strength, but an act of presence. Within the rising heat and biting cold, you’re forced into a parasympathetic reset: tension breaks, the mind steadies, and resilience forms.

This is where recovery becomes ritual.
Where wellness is not a luxury, but a rhythm.

Whether you come after a hard run, a long week, or simply to pause and draw breath — you’ll find a space that welcomes your pace, your level, and your intention. Here, everyone has shelter to return to themselves.

At the height of heat, noise burns off, pressure evaporates — and in that clearing, something shifts.

Born from Scandinavian tradition.
A ritual that transformed us — for the better.
We’re here to share it with you.
